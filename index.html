Privacy Policy

Self Attendance Tracker
Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Self Attendance Tracker collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google for cloud sync, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Attendance Data: Subject names, attendance records (present/absent), timetable entries, classroom details, teacher names, and notes you add.
  • Goals & Preferences: Attendance targets, notification preferences, and app settings.

1.2 Information Stored Locally

All your attendance data is stored on your device using local storage. The app works fully offline without requiring an account.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing attendance tracking functionality
  • Syncing your data across multiple devices (only if you sign in)
  • Sending you class reminders and notifications
  • Calculating attendance statistics and goal progress
  • Improving app performance and user experience

3. Cloud Storage & Sync

If you sign in with your Google account:

  • Your attendance data is stored on Google Firebase servers
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Data is associated with your Google account ID
  • Only you can access your data via your Google login
  • You can disable sync anytime by signing out

4. Notifications

The app sends local notifications for:

  • Class start reminders (configurable timing)
  • "Mark attendance" reminders after class
  • Goal alerts (below/above target attendance)

All notifications are generated locally on your device. You can disable any or all notifications in the app settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

The only third-party service we use is:

  • Google Firebase (for optional cloud sync) — governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Local data is stored in your device's secure storage
  • Cloud data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Firebase uses industry-standard security practices
  • Authentication is handled by Google's secure OAuth system

7. Permissions We Request

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send class and attendance reminders
  • Internet: Only used when you enable cloud sync
  • Vibration: For notification vibrations
  • Schedule alarms: To trigger class notifications at scheduled times

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Edit: Modify any subject, attendance record, or setting
  • Delete: Remove individual records or reset all data via Settings → Reset All Data
  • Export: Download your data as JSON, PDF, or Excel
  • Sign Out: Stop cloud sync anytime
  • Uninstall: Remove the app to delete all local data
  • Account Deletion: Visit our Account Deletion Page for full instructions

9. Children's Privacy

Self Attendance Tracker is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to remove it.

10. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as:

  • The app is installed on your device (for local data)
  • You remain signed in with Google (for cloud data)

When you delete the app or reset data, local data is permanently removed. To delete cloud data, contact us at the email below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
