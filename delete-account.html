Account & Data Deletion Request

Your data, your control. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using one of the methods below.

📱 Method 1: Delete From the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your data is directly from within the app:

1 Open the Self Attendance Tracker app

2 Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner

3 Sign out from Google

Tap your connected Google account → Sign out

4 Go to Settings

From drawer menu → Tap "Settings"

5 Tap "Reset All Data"

Confirm to permanently delete all local data

6 Uninstall the app (optional)

This removes any remaining local data from your device

⚠️ Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your attendance records, subjects, timetable, and goals will be deleted.

📧 Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send us an email and we will delete your data within 7 days:

📧 Send Deletion Request

Or manually send an email to: selfattendancetracker@gmail.com

Include in your email:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Google account email used in the app
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted

🗂️ What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

From Your Device:

  • ✅ All subjects and attendance records
  • ✅ Timetable entries
  • ✅ Goals and preferences
  • ✅ App settings
  • ✅ Cached data

From Our Cloud Servers (Firebase):

  • ✅ Your Google account association
  • ✅ Synced attendance backup
  • ✅ Synced timetable data
  • ✅ Synced goal settings
  • ✅ All metadata (timestamps, etc.)

⏱️ How Long Does It Take?

App deletion: Instant — happens as soon as you tap "Reset All Data" and uninstall.

Email request: Within 7 days of receiving your request.

Backup cleanup: Backups are purged from our systems within 30 days.

🚫 What Data Is NOT Collected?

For transparency, we want you to know we never collect:

  • ❌ Your location
  • ❌ Your contacts
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Device identifiers for ads
  • ❌ Personal photos or media
  • ❌ Any data without your explicit action
